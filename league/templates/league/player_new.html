GZU Winter League will be on 11/29, 12/06, 12/13, 12/20, 12/27 at Guang Ti Sport University. There will be 4 teams, 7 on 7, with adjustments to teams after the first week. Player stats will be kept so that players can judge their progress. Full league rules will be posted on this website.

Please tell your captain each week whether or not you can attend the game, if not, please find a replacement of suitable skill level, however it is the captain's responsbility to field a full team each week.

The cost will be 60 RMB, which will go mostly towards player prizes and a dinner at the end of the season.

The first 60 players to register here will have priority, please register only if you can attend at least 3 games, and preferably all games. Depending on gender ratio, even if you register here we cannot guarantee a spot.

Most importantly: have fun!
